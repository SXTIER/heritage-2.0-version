Victorian Era · c.1880
Diamond Cluster Ring

A magnificent example of Victorian jewellery craftsmanship. The central old mine cut diamond, estimated at 2.8 carats, is surrounded by a cluster of rose-cut diamonds set in 18ct gold with silver collets. The piece retains its original patina and hallmarks consistent with London assay office, circa 1880.

Period Victorian, c.1880
Origin London, England
Metal 18ct Gold & Silver
Stones Old Mine Cut Diamond, Rose-cut Diamonds
